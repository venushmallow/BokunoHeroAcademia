The Series

My Hero Academia is a manga serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump and written by Kōhei Horikoshi.
The story is set in the modern day, except people with special powers have become commonplace throughout the world. Heroes have now filled the world to defeat villains. A boy named Izuku Midoriya has no powers, but he still dreams of becoming a hero one day. Read more...
